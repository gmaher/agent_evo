# Acme Workflow Automation

Acme builds software that automates repetitive computer-based tasks for knowledge workers.
Our product runs on users' desktops and browsers and can:

- Record user actions (clicks, keystrokes, navigation).
- Turn recordings into reusable workflows ("playbooks").
- Integrate with CRMs, spreadsheets, email, and internal web apps.

## Target Users

- Operations managers
- RevOps teams
- Founders and early employees at startups who do a lot of manual ops

## Current Positioning

We emphasize:

- Ease of recording workflows (no coding).
- Reliability and transparency.
- On-device or private-cloud deployment options.
